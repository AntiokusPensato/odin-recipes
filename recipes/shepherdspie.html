Shepherds Pie

Comfort food casserole!

Originally from England. It is traditionally made with mince meat, gravy, begetables, and mashed potatoes. Sometimes it is called "Cottage pie". Although traditionally Shepherds Pie is made with ground lamb and Cottage Pie is made with ground beef.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or lamb
  • Mashed Potatoes
  • Vegetables
  • Flour
  • Ketchup
  • Beef Broth
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make the mashed potatoes
  2. Boild and mash carrots
  3. Cook beef-onion mixture
  4. Assemble the shepherd's pie
  5. Bake the shepherd's pie until the top is golden brown
